Increasing Global Pressure for Sustainable Aviation

Regulators, passengers, investors, and industry bodies are calling for sustainable aviation practices and transparent ESG-aligned environmental reporting across the sector.

Regulatory Authorities in Aviation Sustainability

ICAO's CORSIA scheme, the EU ETS, and the UK Jet Zero Strategy increasingly require airlines and airports to monitor, report, and reduce their carbon emissions.

Aviation Professionals Driving Sustainability

Industry leaders and aviation professionals are championing sustainable aviation fuel (SAF), operational efficiency, and next-generation technology to reduce the sector's climate impact.

Passengers and Communities Demanding Change

73% of air travelers express concern about aviation's environmental impact, with growing demand for carbon-transparent booking and sustainable flight options.
